And It Came To Pass That Coriantumr Wrote Again An Epistle Unto Shiz, Desiring That He Would Not Come Again To Battle, But That He Would Take The Kingdom, And Spare The Lives Of The People. But Behold, The Spirit Of The Lord Had Ceased Striving With Them, And Satan Had Full Power Over The Hearts Of The People, For They Were Given Up Unto The Hardness Of Their Hearts, And The Blindness Of Their Minds That They Might Be Destroyed; Wherefore They Went Again To Battle.
